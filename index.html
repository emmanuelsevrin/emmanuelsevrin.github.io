Me in 10 seconds

During the week, I work as a project manager for the United Nations World Food Programme. You can read about my current work here, and there.

During my free time, I write about inspiration and learning.

I spent the last 10 years of my life travelling and always looking for the next big thing. Now I try to focus on learning and building things useful for others.

I'm a resolute INTJ. People tell me I am analytical, a bit into my own world, and that I overthink things. I've come to consider those things as my way of engaging with the world.
